About this map

Thematic data for this map were derived from two sources:

  • Motor vehicle accident data comes from the Missouri Statewide Traffic Accident Records System (STARS) managed by the Missouri State Highway Patrol.
  • Community-report incidents are collected by members of the community via Esri's Survey123 and managed by the map's author.

Motor vehicle accident data may have a week or two lag in reporting. Community-reported incidents is near-real time; however, reporting began in February 2024, so few incidents prior to that timeframe have been captured. (Note: The community-reported incident data is primarily test data at this stage).

In addition, spatial data for St. Louis's 14 wards, which were updated in 2020, comes from the City of St. Louis Open Data portal.
