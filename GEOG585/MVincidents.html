Motor Vehicle Incidents in St. Louis, Missouri

This page shows motor vehicle crashes and community-reported motor vehicle incidents (CRI) between drivers and bicyclists or pedestrians. Click a ward or an incident to get more information.

Ward risk rating is based on the number of accidents involving bicyclists or pedestrians, taking into account the number of injuries and fatalities, since January 2002.

About this map

Thematic data for this map were derived from two sources:

  • Motor vehicle accident data comes from the Missouri Statewide Traffic Accident Records System (STARS) managed by the Missouri State Highway Patrol.
  • Community-report incidents are collected by members of the community via Esri's Survey123 and managed by the map's author.

Motor vehicle accident data may have a lag in reporting from the date of the accident. Community-reported incidents in this map largely consist of test data recorded in February-March 2024, so few incidents outside of that timeframe have been captured.

In addition, spatial data for St. Louis's 14 wards, which were updated in 2020, comes from the City of St. Louis Open Data portal.
